3. Solving mode

When Gnonograms is in "solving" mode, it displays the "working grid". This grid starts with all grey (unknown) cells.

When in "solving" mode, you can:

  • Solve the puzzle manually by drawing on the grid

  • Check your progress for errors

  • Let the computer solve the puzzle (if it can!)

  • Save a partially finished puzzle

  • Reveal the solution by switching back to design mode
